Interface ModelsDeletedEvent<ModelClass>

Dispatched by a Store when one or more of its models have been deleted. Do not confuse with ModelsDestroyedEvent, which is only dispatched when the models have also been deleted in the associated backend, usually after synchronizing the store that had some models deleted.
